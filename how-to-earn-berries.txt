How to earn Berries (Anisurge)

• Watch anime — finish an episode (~85% watched) → 2 Berries each (max 12/day from watching)
• Daily check-in — Settings → Berries → tap "Claim daily" (login streak = bonus Berries)
• Community chat — first message each day → 1 Berry
• Promo codes — redeem event/Discord codes on the Berries tab

Berries are cosmetic only (profile frames in Store) — not for streaming or downloads.
